Our Mission

Empowering the Nation was founded to provide quality skills training specifically for domestic workers and gardeners. Our mission is to upskill these essential workers, making them more marketable, enabling them to command higher pay, or helping them start their own small businesses.

We believe that by empowering individuals with practical skills and knowledge, we can help build a stronger, more self-sufficient nation.
